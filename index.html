About us

A nursery plant refers to a young, newly grown plant that is cultivated in a controlled environment, typically within a nursery, until it is ready for transplantation into gardens, landscapes, or other growing areas. These plants are carefully nurtured from seeds, cuttings, or small starts and are often sold to consumers for home gardening, landscaping, or agricultural purposes. Nursery plants can include a wide range of species, from flowers and shrubs to trees and vegetables, and are typically chosen for their adaptability, ease of care, and aesthetic appeal.
